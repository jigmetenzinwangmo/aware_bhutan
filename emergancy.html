Emergency Support

Help is available. You are not alone.

💚 Mental Health Support

If you are feeling emotionally overwhelmed, anxious, unsafe, or in crisis, please reach out to trusted adults, family members, counselors, teachers, or emergency services.

Asking for help is a strong and healthy step. Support is important and you deserve care and understanding.
