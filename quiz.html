Daily Wellness Support Quiz

This short quiz helps identify areas where you may need more support, rest, or healthy habits. It is not a medical diagnosis.

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed by daily responsibilities?

2. How well have you been sleeping recently?

3. How often do you take breaks to relax or recharge?

4. Do you feel connected to supportive people in your life?

5. How often do you feel mentally or emotionally tired?
