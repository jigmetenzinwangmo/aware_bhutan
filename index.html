Mental Health Matters

AwareBhutan is a safe online platform created to support Bhutanese children, teenagers, and adults struggling with stress, anxiety, depression, and emotional challenges.

Learn Why We Started Symptoms & Quizzes

Why AwareBhutan Exists

💚 Mental Health Awareness

Many Bhutanese people silently struggle with emotional pain, stress, anxiety, and depression without enough support or awareness.

📈 Rising Suicide Concerns

Bhutan has experienced increasing concerns related to emotional wellbeing, especially among young people and students.

🤝 A Safe Online Space

AwareBhutan was created to provide guidance, support, wellness tips, and educational mental health resources.

Mental health should never be ignored. Everyone deserves support, understanding, and access to resources that can help them feel safe, valued, and hopeful.

How We Help

🧠 Learn Symptoms

Users can learn about common symptoms of anxiety, depression, stress, and emotional struggles.

📝 Self-Check Quizzes

Interactive quizzes help users understand possible mental health symptoms and emotional wellbeing.

🌱 Wellness Tips

Daily wellness guidance encourages healthier habits, emotional balance, and self-care practices.
