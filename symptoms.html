Symptoms & Self-Check Quizzes

Common Symptoms

Anxiety

• Constant worrying
• Nervousness
• Panic feelings
• Trouble sleeping

Depression

• Sadness
• Low energy
• Hopeless feelings
• Loss of interest

Stress

• Irritation
• Headaches
• Feeling overwhelmed
• Difficulty focusing

Social Isolation

• Avoiding others
• Loneliness
• Fear of social situations
• Emotional withdrawal

Burnout

• Constant exhaustion
• Lack of motivation
• Feeling emotionally drained
• Difficulty completing tasks

Low Self-Esteem

• Negative self-talk
• Feeling not good enough
• Fear of failure
• Lack of confidence
