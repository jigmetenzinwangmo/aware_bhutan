Why AwareBhutan Was Created

Supporting mental wellness and emotional wellbeing in Bhutan.

🌱 Our Mission

AwareBhutan was created to spread awareness about mental health, emotional wellbeing, stress, anxiety, and self-care among people in Bhutan.

Many young people, students, and adults struggle silently with stress, pressure, loneliness, and emotional challenges without enough support or safe spaces to talk openly.

This website was built to encourage understanding, reduce stigma, and remind people that mental health matters just as much as physical health.

💚 Why Mental Health Awareness Matters

Emotional struggles can affect anyone regardless of age, background, or lifestyle. Sometimes people may feel overwhelmed by school, relationships, work, social pressure, or personal challenges.

In Bhutan and around the world, many individuals feel afraid to ask for help because of stigma or fear of judgment.

AwareBhutan aims to create a safe online space where users can learn, reflect, and find supportive wellness resources.

“You are never alone. Reaching out for support is a sign of strength.”

🧠 What This Website Offers

✔ Wellness self-check quizzes

✔ Mental health awareness information

✔ Stress management and self-care guidance

✔ Encouragement for healthy conversations

✔ A safe and supportive digital environment
